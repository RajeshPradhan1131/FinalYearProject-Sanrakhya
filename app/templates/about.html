About Sanrakhya 🏥

Sanrakhya is a Health Record and Emergency Assistance System developed to help people find nearby hospitals, check bed availability, and locate blood banks instantly.

This system was built as a Final Year Project with the goal of using technology to save lives during critical medical emergencies. It combines hospital data, real-time updates, and an AI-powered chatbot to guide users.

Our Mission 🎯

To provide quick and reliable medical assistance, reduce delays in emergencies, and make healthcare information accessible at your fingertips.

Technology Stack 💻

Flask (Python) | Bootstrap | HTML/CSS/JS | SQLite/MySQL
